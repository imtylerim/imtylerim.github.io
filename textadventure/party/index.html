You open the door of an unfamiliar house. Immediately the beat hits you.

Except for the flashing of strobe lights reds, greens and blues, the house is dark. You peer into the left, where vaguely human shapes are jumping around in the living room.

Straight across from you, in what looks like the kitchen and dining room, people are standing with cups in their hands, shouting through the music, or leaning on the wall to check their phones.

To the right, there's a staircase going up into the house.
